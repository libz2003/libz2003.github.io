I really learnt a lot from Ross program, one of the most impressive things is the proof for Lagrange 4-squares theorem.

Statement

Theorem 1.1: $\forall n\in \mathbb{Z}^+,\exists a,b,c,d\in \mathbb{Z}\ s.t.$ $$n=a^2+b^2+c^2+d^2$$
